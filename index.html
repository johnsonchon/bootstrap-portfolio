About Me

I am currently a full-time Digital Marketing Specialist at MW Polar. I graduated from Cal State Fullerton with a Bachelors in Marketing. I began dabbling in graphic design in 2006 and completely fell in love. In 2008 I grew my passion into a small side business in men’s street-wear. With a partner, we started our venture into the e-commerce world. Soon, my love for business and branding drew me in to study business at California State University, Fullerton.
